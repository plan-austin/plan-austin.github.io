# Who We Are

The Parmer Lane Alliance of Neighborhoods is a group of HOAs, associations and other individuals. 

We are a group of neighbors who are seeking to ensure that the Parmer Lane project will deliver the quality and value that our community deserves.


## Our Mission

- To spread awareness of the Parmer Lane project
- To provide a platform for community input and feedback
- Ensure that we do the best to accomodate the need ands wants of all who use and live along the corridor
